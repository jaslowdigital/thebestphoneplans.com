About Verizon Wireless

Verizon Wireless is one of America's largest wireless telecommunications providers with approximately 146 million subscribers.

Known for its extensive nationwide coverage and reliable service, Verizon operates one of the most robust networks in the country. Their 5G Ultra Wideband network continues to expand, offering some of the fastest mobile data speeds available in supported areas.

Frequently Asked Questions

Verizon postpaid plans typically offer more premium features like priority data access, more generous hotspot allowances, and additional perks like Disney+ and Apple Music. They also include traditional customer service via stores, phone, and chat.

Visible is Verizon's digital-first brand that uses the same network but with simplified plans, lower prices, and customer service primarily through their app. Visible plans include taxes and fees in their pricing, while Verizon postpaid adds these charges on top of advertised rates.

Most unlocked phones manufactured after 2018 will work with Verizon and its MVNOs, but compatibility varies by device. Here's how to check:

  1. For Verizon: Visit Verizon's device compatibility checker on their website and enter your IMEI number
  2. For Visible: Use the compatibility checker in the Visible app or website
  3. For Total Wireless and other MVNOs: Check the specific MVNO's website for compatibility tools

Generally, phones need to support VoLTE and Verizon's specific LTE bands (especially bands 13, 4, and 66) for optimal service.

Verizon's 5G network comes in two varieties:

  • 5G Ultra Wideband: Offers extremely fast speeds (up to 3Gbps) but limited coverage, typically in urban areas and large venues. This uses mmWave and C-band spectrum.
  • 5G Nationwide: More widely available but offers speeds comparable to 4G LTE.

Compared to competitors:

  • T-Mobile currently offers the most extensive 5G coverage and consistent mid-band performance
  • AT&T's 5G offers a balance of coverage and speed
  • Verizon's Ultra Wideband can be the fastest when available, but coverage is more limited

Verizon postpaid plans include various perks depending on the plan level:

  • Disney Bundle: Disney+, Hulu, and ESPN+ included with most unlimited plans
  • Apple Music: 6 months included with some plans, full subscription with higher-tier plans
  • Apple One: Included with Ultimate plan
  • Gaming perks: Apple Arcade or Google Play Pass included with select plans
  • Travel benefits: International calling, texting, and data allowances
  • Cloud storage: Expanded storage options

Visible and other MVNOs typically offer fewer perks but at lower monthly costs.
